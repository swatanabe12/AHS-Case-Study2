Animal Humane Society Web Redesign

Easily navigable, fresh, fun, and ultimately helping animals find their forever homes.

Timeline 3 weeks

Team Alec Friedman, Alex Monson, April Safford, Hayley Grant, Sydney Watanabe

Role User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, UI Design, User Testing

Project Overview

As part of a team made up of 5 people, we all collaborated as UX UI designers and researchers to bring a new perspective to the website. We felt that the call-to-action (CTA) needed to be more defined, and the overall goal was to improve the adoption process.

The Problem

Bringing a new pet into the family is always an exciting time, however, it takes a lot of research, time, and patience to find the perfect fit. With so many animal profiles to look through online or on apps, the options and information is often overwhelming to sift through. When adopting an animal from a shelter, there are often cases of those animals being returned due to the lack of knowledge on behavioral or medical problems.

The Solution

Create a ‘Perfect Match Quiz’in order to filter the user’s needs when searching for a pet; once they find an animal they are interested in based on the quiz results, the animal’s profile will provideaccurate, transparent information on behavior and health status.
